Polar Bear

Polar bears have an excellent sense of smell, with the ability to detect seals nearly a mile away(1.6 km).

  • Scientific Name: Ursus Maritimus
  • Weight: Female: 150-250kg, Male: 450kg
  • Height: Female: 2m, Male: 1.3m
  • Speed: 40kmph
  • Habitat: Circumpolar Arctic

It is the largest carnivore that lives on land.Polar bears use sea ice as a platform to hunt seals.Seals make up most of a polar bears diet. Polar bears keep warm thanks to nearly 10 cm of blubber under the skin. The polar bear was the mascot for the 1988 Winter Olympics in Calgary,Canada.
